ABOUT MAURITIUS

Mauritius was named by Prince Maurits Van Nassau during the Dutch settlement in 1598. Mauritius is a small Island located off the south east coast of the Africa continent in the Indian Oceans. The island is situated approximately 2400 KM of the east coast of Africa and it covers an area of 1865 square km. Mauritius is 65KM in length and 45KM in width.

Population

Mauritius is a densely populated island of about 1.2 millions of people. It is a multi-cutural country where all the ethnic groups present such as Hindu, Muslims, Creoles, Chinese and Europeans.
