SOUTH TOUR

During the visit you will start your tours by visiting the Ship model Factory, Adamas Diamond Factory, where you get opportunity to do shopping also, these 2 shops is situated in the town of Curepipe. After Vising the factory, you will proceed to the Trou Aux Cerfs Volcano which is also found in Curepipe. After visiting the Volcano Crater, you will proceed to Grand Bassin the Natural Lake, where Hindus of the country go for Pilgrimage. Then, We will take you to the Black River Gorges Natural Park View Point, where you will get the chance to breath the fresh air and take Pictures for Souvenir. After visiting the Gorge, the last stop will be Chamarel Fall, where you will witness the 7 unique colored earths, and also you will be able to buy the best coffee of Mauritius.

SOUTH TOUR PLACES OF INTEREST

  • Ship Model Factory
  • Adamas Diamond Factory
  • Trou Aux Cerf
  • Ganga Talao ~ Grand-Bassin
  • Black River Gorges Natural Park
  • 7-Coloured Earth ~ Chamarel
